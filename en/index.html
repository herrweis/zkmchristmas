GLOBALE

The New Art Event: 300 Days at the ZKM 2015 - 2016

300 years of Karlsruhe – 300 days of GLOBALE. On the occasion of the city’s anniversary celebrations the ZKM | Karlsruhe is poised to present a new art event: the GLOBALE makes visible the cultural effects of globalization while at the same time laying bare the connections between art, science and technology – from the digital revolution to the development of democracy. Exhibitions, concerts, performances, productions and readings all illustrate the ways in which art and science begin to approximate one another by way of new technologies. Developments and productions in art, design, architecture, music, media, natural science, technology, economy, ecology, etc. open up new perspectives to 21st century society.
